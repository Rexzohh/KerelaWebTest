About Kera-La-Carte

Wellington's only restaurant serving authentic food from Kerala, South India. Our food is chock-full of Kerala deliciousness, dishing out some serious flavour-packed eats straight from our heritage. Our food is flavourful and not all chilli hot. Well-priced dishes. Groups welcome. Comprehensive menu includes vegan, vegetarian, meat, fish, poultry, DF and GF choices.
